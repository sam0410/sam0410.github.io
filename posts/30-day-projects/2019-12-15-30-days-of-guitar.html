Personal Project 2 : 30 Days of Guitar.


December 15, 2019

Before the challenge :
I have always wanted to learn guitar, so I gave it a shot in my second year of college. I can play basic songs and I can switch between basic chords. But till now, I am unable to play the barre chords. This challenge is to improve my guitar skills. I am trying to balance between a lot of things in my life lately, but as they say :

Nobody is too busy, it's a matter of priorities.

The plan : Practice guitar everyday for atleast 20 minutes.


After the challenge :
[15th January, 2020 - One month later]

It is amazing how just 20 minutes a day for 30 days improved my skills so much. Not only did I learn to play the barre chords but also learnt a new rhythm called "Rumba Flamenco". Here is a small clip potraiying my progress in this 30 day challenge :


Now, I can also play my favourite Hindi song "Hai Junoon" by KK on guitar : I couldn't do this earlier as it involves barre chords. Although, I am still not expert in playing Guitar : But, overall, I am really happy with the progress I made in this challenge.
